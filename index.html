Biography

I’m a postdoctoral researcher at Interactive Geometry Lab in ETH Zurich with Professor Olga Sorkine-Hornung. I earned by BSc ans MSc in the area of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science from Lomonosov Moscow State university. Later, I received my Ph.D. Degree from LAVA Lab, KAIST, under supervision of Professor Sung-Hee Lee.

My research interests revolve around the application of AI to geometry processing, shape generation, and reconstruction, specifically in the areas of Digital garments and Virtual humans. I find inspiration in science, creative fields, and stunning visual masterpieces.
